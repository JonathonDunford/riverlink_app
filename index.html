RiverLink is pleased to offer you this app as a one stop shop for the French Broad River watershed. Enjoy the river, wear your life jacket, and remember that the French Broad has a mighty current, so be careful, professional and fully conscious every minute on the river. You are responsible for everything you do on or near this wonderful resource. RiverLink invites you and your family and friends to responsibly enjoy the river, but accepts no responsibility for you or your family and friends while you are utilizing the French Broad, this app, or our website at riverlink.org. RiverLink strongly recommends you NEVER drink alcoholic beverages while enjoying the French Broad river. If you are having a life threatening experience, please call 911 immediately. Thank you and enjoy.
